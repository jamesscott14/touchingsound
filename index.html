Journey Through the World of Sound Creation

TOUCHING SOUND

Music is both a pursuit and a practice of the mind, body and spirit.

Touching Sound is a site designed to bring together those dedicated individuals, from all genres of music, involved in this pursuit and practice of sound creation.

The Mind Body Connection

There are two aspects of individual harmony:

"The harmony between body and soul, and the harmony between individuals. All the tragedy in the world, in the individual and in the multitude, comes from lack of harmony. And harmony is the best given by producing harmony in one's own life." - Hazrat Inayat Khan

The Practice

Throughout the centuries human beings have discovered and created a multitude of ways to bring us closer to sound. Those approaches have been codified over the millenia and are now at our disposal.

  • Melody
  • Harmony
  • Rhythm

The Tools

When it comes to approaching the world of music there are few things we must keep in mind in order to better guarantee more positive results for the time and effort we give to it. Let's take a closer look...

  • Patience
  • Open Ears
  • A willingness to make mistakes
  • Compassion towards oneself and others
